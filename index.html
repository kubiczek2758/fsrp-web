Server Rules

Rules that apply to all players

  1. Respect other players and staff — zero tolerance for toxicity, discrimination, or harassment.
  2. Play realistically — avoid fail RP, meta-gaming, power-gaming, and random deathmatch (RDM).
  3. Use only official in-game communication channels during roleplay (in-game voice).
  4. Do not abuse game or script bugs for personal gain — report them to staff.
  5. Follow instructions from staff and uniformed service members within roleplay.
  6. Keep your account secure and do not share it with others — you are responsible for your actions.
  7. Cheats, mod menus, and any unauthorized software are strictly prohibited.
  8. Remember: the goal is fun for everyone — play responsibly!
