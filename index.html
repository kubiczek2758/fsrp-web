Server Rules

Our community standards ensure immersive, fair, and enjoyable roleplay for everyone. Read them carefully before joining.

📋

General Conduct

  1. Treat all players, staff, and content creators with respect at all times.
  2. Zero tolerance for racism, sexism, homophobia, harassment, threats, or hate speech.
  3. Do not spam chat, voice channels, or in-game communications.
  4. Advertising other servers, products, or services is not allowed without staff approval.
  5. Keep your account secure. You are responsible for all actions taken on your account.
  6. Do not share personal information of other players without consent.
  7. Follow all instructions given by staff members. Their decisions are final.
🎭

Roleplay Standards

  1. Stay in character at all times while in-game. Do not break character over voice or text.
  2. Play realistically — your actions should reflect real-life consequences and common sense.
  3. Fail RP is prohibited. This includes unrealistic driving, escaping custody unrealistically, or ignoring injuries.
  4. Meta-gaming is not allowed. Do not use information obtained outside of your character's knowledge.
  5. Power-gaming is prohibited. You must allow others to react to your roleplay actions.
  6. RDM / VDM — killing or harming players without valid in-character reason is not allowed.
  7. Use in-game voice chat only during active roleplay sessions.
  8. Value your character's life. Act with fear and self-preservation in dangerous situations.
🚔

Crime & Combat

  1. Crime must be roleplayed realistically. Plan heists, robberies, and illegal activities with story in mind.
  2. Hostage situations require a valid reason and must be handled seriously by all sides.
  3. Do not shoot on sight. Verbal interaction and escalation are required before lethal force.
  4. After a serious injury, you must roleplay the consequences — respawn does not erase memory of the events leading to death.
  5. Police and emergency personnel must be given reasonable time to respond and roleplay scenes.
  6. Gang wars and large-scale conflict must be approved or organized through staff.
💻

Technical & Fair Play

  1. Cheats, hacks, mod menus, macros, or any unauthorized third-party software are strictly forbidden.
  2. Do not abuse bugs, glitches, or exploits. Report them to staff immediately.
  3. Do not use modified game files that provide unfair advantages.
  4. Do not intentionally crash, lag, or disrupt the server or other players.
  5. Alt accounts are not allowed without explicit staff permission.
💬

Discord & Community

  1. Follow Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  2. Use the appropriate channels for each topic.
  3. Tag staff only when necessary. Do not DM staff members without permission.
  4. Department applications must be filled out honestly and completely.
  5. Streaming and content creation are welcome, but follow server streaming rules.
⚖️

Final Note

These rules exist to protect the experience for everyone. Staff reserve the right to take action against any behavior that damages the community, even if not explicitly listed above. The goal is simple: create amazing stories together, respect each other, and have fun.

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the server depending on severity.
